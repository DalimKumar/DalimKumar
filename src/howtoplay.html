Narco Lore

The time is now, Neo Narcos are ready for the invasion. The Chosen One, an inter-dimensional traveller from 1bn lizard years away has declared siege on Kratos; capturing its land, seizing its $Spice fields, milking its fortune and taking control of its people.

You play the role of one of the genesis narcos, the coolest bad guys in the galaxy. There are four crime families who control the planet Kratos, with varying strengths and skills - Collect them all, earn $Spice, upgrade your army and repel the invasion.

Stake your Narco army in the invasion to yield $Spice. Bribe the Chosen One to bank your loot, or build your street cred to keep it all.

Genesis Narcos

There are four crime families who share control of the planet Kratos, each with their own skills. They will need to put aside their differences and fight together against the coming invasion.

By staking your Narcos into the invasion effort you yield $Spice. All staked Narcos will earn 2x the daily $Spice yield for the first 48 hours of the start of the staking game. Our aim is for all staked Narco owners to be able to access all future drops for free with the claimed $Spice.
